How to Pizza, French Toast

In this iteration of How to Pizza, I'll be discussing how to make French Toast and what they are.

Now, what are french toast? French toast, (also known as "eggy bread") is a delightful breakfast dish made from sliced bread that's soaked in a mixture of beaten eggs (often whisked with a milk or cream) and then pan-fried until golden and crispy. Lets go through the steps in making this comforting food:

  1. Ingredients:
    • Sliced bread (day-old bread works well)
    • Eggs (usually 2-3 per serving)
    • Milk or cream (about 1/4 cup per egg)
    • Butter or oil for frying
    • Optional: Sugar(powdered), cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla for flavor
  2. Preperation:
    • Whisk the Eggs: Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them well. Add a splash of milk or cream and any desired flavorings (such as vanilla or cinnamon) and mx until combined.
    • Soak the Bread: Dip each slice into the egg mixture, ensuring both sides are coated. Let the bread soak for a few seconds to absorb the liquid.
    • Heat the Pan: Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Add a knob of butter or drizzle of oil
    • Fry the Bread: Place the soaked bread slices in the pan. Cook until golden brown on one side (about 2-3 minutes), thenflip and cook the other side.
    • Serve Hot: French Toast is best enjoyes warm. Top it with your favorite toppings as listed below.
  3. Toppings and Variations:
    • Classic Sweet French Toasts:
      • Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
      • Drizzle with maple syrup or honey.
      • Add fresh berries, sliced bannanas, or a dollop of whipped cream.
    • Savory French Toast:
      • Skip the sugar and add a pinch of salt adn pepper to the egg mixture.
      • Serve with crispy bacon, sauteed spinach, or avocado slices.
    • Creative Twist:
      • Use different types of bread: brioche, challah, or whole-grain slices.
      • Stuff the Frech toast with cream cheese and jam for a decadent treat as a sandwich.
      • Make a French toast sandwich with ham and cheese.
      • Try savory herbs like thyme or rosemary in the egg mixture.

The name, "French Toast" has an intersting history. Believed that impoverished European cooks in the Middle Ages invented this dish to use up stale bread. They moistened the bread with eggs, fried it, and boom French Toast was born!!
